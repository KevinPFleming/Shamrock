MUSIC LESSONS FOR ALL AGES!

Whether you are age 5, 85, or anything in between, the teachers at Shamrock can teach you how to play and enjoy music. Instuments include Guitar, Bass Guitar, Banjo, Mandolin, Ukulele, Lap Steel, and even Music Theory. Lessons are available in private settings and in classes with others.

JAM CAMPS FOR KIDS!

Wanna join a band? Or just learn how to jam with others? Shamrock Jam Camps are where its at! No matter what instrument you play, we want YOU to join us for a good time. Whether you just want a casual jam session or you are looking to form and run a band, Shamrock delivers!
